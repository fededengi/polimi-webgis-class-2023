About the area

The analysis was conducted within the boundaries of the Val Belviso area, a glacial valley of Middle Valtellina, between the municipalities of Teglio and Aprica, in the province of Sondrio, Lombardy. It is best known for the presence of the Frera Dam, which, behind it, generated the Belviso artificial lake.
